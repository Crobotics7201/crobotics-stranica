# Guests from Sarajevo

This week we had the opportunity to host students from the First Gymnasium Sarajevo. They had the chance to attend a presentation about our robotics competitions.

Our students presented our school robot to the guests from Sarajevo. We also talked to them about the competitions we have participated in, the places we have travelled to, and the results we achieved. The guests listened with great interest to our experiences and achievements.
