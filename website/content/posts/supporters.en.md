# You Allow Us to Reach the Moon

Dear supporters,

We hope this message finds you well. On behalf of our robotics team, we would like to express our sincerest gratitude for your support as we compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition.

Your donations are critical to our success and allow us to pursue our mission of competing and becoming better year by year. Your generosity directly impacts our ability to purchase necessary materials and equipment, and bring our robots to life in this competitive arena.

We are proud to have such a dedicated community of supporters and we will continue to work tirelessly to deliver on our mission. We would like to invite you to visit our blog to learn more about our work and stay updated on our progress in the competition.

Thank you again for your support. Your generosity truly makes a difference in our work and we couldn't be more grateful.

Best regards, Crobotics.

# Our Supporters
