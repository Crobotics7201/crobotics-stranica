As our departure for Chicago approaches, we are working more and more, preparing and refining our robot so that we can give our maximum at the competition. Behind every success, every hour spent in the workshop, and every result achieved stands the great effort of the entire team, as well as the support of those without whom none of this would be possible. 

 

Our work is largely made possible by the Primorje-Gorski Kotar County and the Ministry of Science and Education, who are our main donors and long-standing partners. Their continuous support enables us to acquire equipment, participate in competitions, and continuously develop and improve our projects. 

 

We would also like to extend special thanks to the Faculty of Engineering in Rijeka, which this year supported us with a donation of robot parts. This support was extremely important in our preparations and significantly contributed to the quality of our robot. 

 

Thanks to this kind of support, as well as all the other support we have received over the years, we have the opportunity to learn, grow, develop our skills, and present our work on a global level. 

 

Thank you for believing in us and for helping us achieve our dreams!
